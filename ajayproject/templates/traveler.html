Famous Places to Visit in Ujjain

Mahakaleshwar Temple

Distance: 0 km (City Center) | Visit Time: 6:00 AM – 12:00 PM, 4:00 PM – 9:00 PM

One of the 12 Jyotirlingas of Lord Shiva — the spiritual heart of Ujjain.

Ram Ghat

Distance: 1 km | Visit Time: 5:00 AM – 8:00 PM

Sacred bathing site on the Shipra River — famous for Kumbh Mela and evening aarti.

Kal Bhairav Temple

Distance: 7 km | Visit Time: 6:00 AM – 9:00 PM

Dedicated to Lord Bhairav — known for unique liquor offerings to the deity.

Sandipani Ashram

Distance: 3 km | Visit Time: 8:00 AM – 6:00 PM

Where Lord Krishna, Balarama, and Sudama studied under Guru Sandipani.

Harsiddhi Temple

Distance: 1 km | Visit Time: 6:00 AM – 10:00 PM

A Shakti Peetha — famous for its twin lamp pillars lit during Navratri.

Bhartrihari Caves

Distance: 4 km | Visit Time: 7:00 AM – 7:00 PM

Ancient meditation caves believed to be where poet-saint Bhartrihari lived.

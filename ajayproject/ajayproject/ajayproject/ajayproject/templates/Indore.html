Explore Indore – The Heart of Madhya Pradesh

Your gateway to culture, food, and heritage

Why Visit Indore?

Indore is a vibrant city known for its rich cultural heritage, mouthwatering street food, and architectural beauty. It blends modernity with history, making it a must-visit destination in central India.

Top Places to Visit

  • Rajwada Palace

  • Lal Bagh Palace

  • Khajrana Ganesh Temple

  • Sarafa Bazaar (Night Food Market)

  • ranjeet hanuman mandir

Local Cuisine

Don’t miss out on Poha-Jalebi for breakfast, and head to Sarafa and Chhappan Dukan for evening snacks like Bhutte ka Kees and Garadu.

How to Reach

Indore is well-connected via air, rail, and road. Devi Ahilya Bai Holkar Airport receives flights from major cities in India.

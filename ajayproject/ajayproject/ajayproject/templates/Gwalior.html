About Gwalior

Gwalior is a historic city in Madhya Pradesh known for its majestic fort, ancient temples, and rich cultural heritage. It's a perfect blend of history and modernity, making it a must-visit destination for travelers.

Top Attractions

  • Gwalior Fort – A hilltop fort known for its grand architecture and panoramic views.
  • Jai Vilas Palace – A European-style palace with a museum showcasing royal artifacts.
  • Sas Bahu Temples – Ancient temples with exquisite carvings.
  • Tansen Tomb – Memorial of the legendary musician Tansen.

How to Reach

By Air: Gwalior has a domestic airport with flights from major cities.

By Train: Well connected by rail to Delhi, Mumbai, Bhopal, and other major cities.

By Road: Regular bus and taxi services from nearby cities.

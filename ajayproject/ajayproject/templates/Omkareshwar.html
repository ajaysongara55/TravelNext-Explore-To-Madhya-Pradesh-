About Omkareshwar

Omkareshwar is a sacred island shaped like the Hindu "Om" symbol, located on the Narmada River in Madhya Pradesh. It is home to one of the 12 revered Jyotirlinga shrines of Lord Shiva, attracting pilgrims and travelers alike.

Top Attractions

Omkareshwar Temple

An ancient temple dedicated to Lord Shiva, located on the Om-shaped island.

Mamleshwar Temple

A temple situated across the bridge, considered just as sacred as Omkareshwar itself.

Narmada River

Enjoy serene boat rides and spiritual ambience on the holy Narmada River.

About Pachmarhi

Pachmarhi, located in Madhya Pradesh, is a beautiful hill station known for its lush greenery, waterfalls, caves, and colonial charm. It's a UNESCO Biosphere Reserve nestled in the Satpura range.

Top Attractions

Bee Falls

A scenic waterfall that's a must-visit for nature lovers and photography enthusiasts.

Jatashankar Caves

Natural caves with religious significance and fascinating rock formations.

Dhoopgarh

The highest point in Madhya Pradesh, offering spectacular sunrise and sunset views.

About Kuno National Park

Kuno National Park, located in the Sheopur district of Madhya Pradesh, is one of India's most promising wildlife conservation areas. Recently in the spotlight for being the site where cheetahs were reintroduced to India, Kuno is home to diverse fauna, lush forests, and a revitalized ecosystem.

Wildlife Highlights

  • Reintroduced African Cheetahs
  • Leopards
  • Sloth Bears
  • Striped Hyenas
  • Chinkara, Nilgai, and Sambhar Deer
  • Over 200 species of birds

Safari and Visit Information

Safaris in Kuno are regulated and must be booked through the Madhya Pradesh Forest Department's website or authorized agents. The park is open from October to June, with the best sightings in winter and early summer.

How to Reach

Nearest Airport: Gwalior Airport (approx. 160 km)
Nearest Railway Station: Sheopur or Gwalior
By Road: Well-connected via highways from Gwalior, Shivpuri, and Morena

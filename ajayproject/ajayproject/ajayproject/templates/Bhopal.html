About Bhopal

Bhopal, the capital city of Madhya Pradesh, is known for its natural beauty, rich history, and blend of Hindu and Islamic cultures. It's famous for its lakes, palaces, and mosques.

Top Attractions

Taj-ul-Masajid

One of the largest mosques in India with stunning architecture and a serene ambiance.

Bharat Bhavan

A multi-arts complex that promotes visual and performing arts in Bhopal.

Upper Lake

A beautiful man-made lake offering boating and scenic views of the city.

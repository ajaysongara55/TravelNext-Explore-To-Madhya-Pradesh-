Discover Rewa - Nature, History, and Heritage

About Rewa

Rewa, located in northeastern Madhya Pradesh, is a hidden gem rich in historical significance and natural beauty. Known as the land of the White Tiger, Rewa offers majestic waterfalls, ancient forts, and a vibrant cultural heritage.

Top Attractions

  • Keoti Falls – One of the tallest waterfalls in India
  • Rewa Fort – A 19th-century fort with royal architecture
  • Govindgarh Palace – Home of the first white tiger in the wild
  • Purwa Falls – A serene spot for nature lovers
  • Chachai Falls – Stunning views and peaceful surroundings

Best Time to Visit

The ideal time to visit Rewa is from October to March when the weather is pleasant and perfect for sightseeing and exploring the waterfalls and wildlife.

How to Reach

By Air: The nearest airport is Prayagraj (Allahabad), around 130 km away.

By Train: Rewa is well-connected by rail to major cities like Bhopal, Jabalpur, and Delhi.

By Road: Rewa is accessible via highways from Satna, Jabalpur, and Varanasi.

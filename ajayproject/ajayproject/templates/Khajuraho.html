About Khajuraho

Khajuraho, located in the Chhatarpur district of Madhya Pradesh, is famous for its stunning group of Hindu and Jain temples known for their intricate sculptures and erotic carvings. A UNESCO World Heritage Site, it reflects the rich culture, art, and architectural brilliance of ancient India.

Top Attractions

  • Kandariya Mahadev Temple
  • Lakshmana Temple
  • Vishwanath Temple
  • Jain Temples of the Eastern Group
  • Sound and Light Show at Western Group

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Khajuraho is from October to March when the weather is pleasant. The annual Khajuraho Dance Festival in February is a cultural highlight showcasing Indian classical dances in the temple backdrop.

Getting There

Khajuraho is well connected by air, rail, and road. The nearest airport is Khajuraho Airport (HJR), and the town has its own railway station. Regular buses and taxis are available from major cities of Madhya Pradesh.

About Vidisha

Vidisha, located near Bhopal in Madhya Pradesh, is a city rich in history and archaeological significance. It was an important trade and cultural center during the Mauryan and Gupta periods.

Top Attractions

Heliodorus Pillar

Also known as the Khamba Baba, this 2nd-century BCE pillar was erected by a Greek ambassador and is one of the earliest known Hindu religious monuments.

Udayagiri Caves

These ancient rock-cut caves house beautiful Gupta-era sculptures and inscriptions, including the famous Varaha (boar) avatar of Vishnu.

Bijamandal Mosque

Ruins of a mosque built on the foundations of a Hindu temple, offering a glimpse into the layered history of Vidisha.

Travel Tips

  • Best time to visit: November to February
  • How to reach: Well-connected by road and rail from Bhopal (approx. 60 km)
  • Don’t miss: Local sweets and handicrafts in Vidisha markets
